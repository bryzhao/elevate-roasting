Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh is your coffee?

We roast all coffee to order, ensuring maximum freshness. Coffee is typically shipped within 24-48 hours of roasting.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we ship to all 50 states. Shipping is free on orders over $50.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Absolutely! You can pause, modify, or cancel your subscription at any time through your account.

What's your return policy?

If you're not satisfied with your coffee, we'll gladly replace it or provide a full refund within 30 days.
