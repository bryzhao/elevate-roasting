Our Story

Started in a San Francisco apartment with a popcorn popper and curiosity about what makes great coffee actually great.

How it started

It began with a $20 popcorn popper from Goodwill and green beans ordered online. Roasting on the fire escape of a small SF apartment, logging every variable, obsessing over first crack timing and development ratios.

What started as curiosity became a pursuit of understanding: how origin, processing, roast development, and extraction all interact to create something worth sharing.

What we do now

We source green coffee directly from farmers and cooperatives, focusing on traceable lots with detailed processing information. Each roast is logged, profiled, and cupped to ensure consistency.

Small batches mean we can focus on quality over quantity. Every bag includes roast date, origin details, and brewing recommendations based on actual testing, not marketing copy.

What matters to us

Traceability

Know where your coffee comes from, how it was processed, and who grew it.

Consistency

Logged roast profiles and systematic approach to ensure repeatable quality.

Honesty

Actual tasting notes, real roast dates, transparent pricing with no marketing fluff.
