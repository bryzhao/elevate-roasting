Espresso Brewing Guide

Espresso Machine

  • Dose: 18-21g ground coffee
  • Yield: 36-42g espresso
  • Time: 25-30 seconds
  • Temperature: 90-96°C (195-205°F)
  • Pressure: 9 bars

Alternative Methods

  • Moka Pot: Fine grind, 1:7 ratio
  • AeroPress: Fine grind, 1:6 ratio, 2 minutes
  • Pour Over: Medium-fine grind, 1:16 ratio
  • French Press: Coarse grind, 1:15 ratio, 4 minutes
