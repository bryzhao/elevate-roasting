Single Origin Coffee

Each single origin coffee tells a story of its unique terroir, climate, and the people who cultivate it. Discover the distinct flavors that make each origin special.

What Makes Single Origin Special?

Single origin coffees come from one specific region, farm, or even a single lot. This allows the unique characteristics of that particular terroir to shine through.

Each origin has its own distinct flavor profile influenced by factors like altitude, soil composition, climate, and processing methods. From the bright acidity of Kenyan coffees to the chocolate notes of Colombian beans, every origin offers a unique tasting experience.

Flavor Profiles by Region

  • Africa: Bright, fruity, floral notes
  • Central America: Balanced, clean, citrus acidity
  • South America: Nutty, chocolate, caramel sweetness
  • Asia Pacific: Earthy, spicy, full-bodied
